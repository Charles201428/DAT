Bitcoin Bounce Stalls as XRP, Zcash Lead Gains; Arca Says Rally Not a Dead-Cat Bounce
The recent momentum in the cryptocurrency market has seen Bitcoin's rally experience a slowdown, causing its price to stabilize after a period of significant fluctuation. Meanwhile, alternative cryptocurrencies such as XRP and Zcash have emerged as leaders, posting notable gains and attracting the attention of investors. In light of these developments, the investment firm Arca has expressed a positive outlook, asserting that the current rally in the crypto market should not be dismissed as a fleeting phenomenon or a mere 'dead-cat bounce,' but rather as a sign of sustained interest and potential growth in the space.
